About Me

I build the bridge between market intelligence, investment strategy, and execution for AI data center energy infrastructure. At Passkey (SK Group's US clean energy platform), I lead strategy and origination for data center power investments — evaluating grid-tied and behind-the-meter solutions, building financial models, and turning complex grid realities into financeable development plans.

My path here was anything but linear. I started at Boston Consulting Group advising telcos and industrial clients on billion-dollar strategy decisions. At Google, I managed a ~$30M ad sales portfolio. I then founded a startup, earned my MBA at UC Berkeley with a focus on sustainable business, and joined X (Google's moonshot factory) to work on climate tech commercialization.

What ties it all together: I thrive at the intersection of strategy, partnerships, and execution — turning ambiguous, high-stakes problems into structured plans that move. I'm currently exploring my next opportunity in data center energy infrastructure.
